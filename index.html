    So Google renamed Bard, its AI model, to become Gemini. I was intrigued that Gemini could generate images for free. And so I fancied using Gemini over Chat GPT for personal stuff. It cannot generate human images because of a fault that made it unable to generate images of white people. But it could generate images of other things, like this Nwanza.

    The prompt that generated this beautiful image did not have 'generate the image of a bird' in it. I had wanted a picture that portrayed variation in maths, and Gemini decided to give me a bird with varying colours amongst other suggestions. So I thought, 'Why not design and build a website inspired by the Nwanza - the bird in the Igbo stories.'

    So here we go! Welcome to Stanley's Nwanza!
